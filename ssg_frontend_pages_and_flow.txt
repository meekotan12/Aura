SSG Flow (Frontend Perspective)

1. School IT creates SSG roles for a school.
2. School IT assigns permissions to each role.
3. School IT assigns students to roles per school year.
4. Students with SSG roles see extra SSG action cards on their dashboard.
5. SSG members with create_event permission create events (status = pending).
6. SSG approvers with approve_event permission approve or reject events.
7. Approved events appear in Student dashboards as Upcoming Events (next 7 days).
8. 24 hours before an approved event, students receive an in-app notification.
9. Students open the notification bell to read and mark notifications as read.
10. SSG members with post_announcement permission create announcements; all students in the school can view them.
